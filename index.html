About Me

Over the past few years, I have faced some health challenges that have caused me to reevaluate my career path. While I have always been passionate about teaching and working with students, my health issues have made it increasingly difficult for me to maintain the level of energy and physical presence required in the classroom. After careful consideration, I have decided to make a career change into software engineering.

I believe that this transition aligns better with my health needs because it offers a more flexible work environment and allows me to work remotely when necessary. Additionally, I have always had an interest in technology and coding, and I see this as an opportunity to leverage my problem-solving skills and passion for learning in a new and exciting field. I am excited about this new chapter in my career and am dedicated to continuing my professional growth and contributing positively to the software engineering community.
